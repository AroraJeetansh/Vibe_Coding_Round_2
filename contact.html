FAQ's

What makes Inlighn Tech different from other learning platforms?
Inlighn Tech provides top-quality learning modules and projects based on the latest industry technologies and trends.
How can I register for an internship?
Go to the Internships section and click on Apply Now.
After filling out the registration form, whom should I contact?
Once you complete your registration, you will receive a WhatsApp group invitation for your batch, where further updates will be shared. You can also contact us via WhatsApp at 9368842663.
What is the project submission deadline?
Your project submission deadline will be the same as your internship completion date. You must submit your project before the completion date.
Is it mandatory to submit a project only from the available options on the portal?
No, it is not mandatory to submit a project only from the available options. You can submit your own project as long as it is relevant to your internship domain.
How can I proceed with signing up at Inlighn Tech?
Once you complete the Apply Now process, you will receive access to your internship portal via email. From there, you can sign up for your account.
Can I earn a certificate after completing an internship at Inlighn Tech?
Yes, once you complete all the learning modules in your internship portal, you will receive a completion certificate.
Can I interact with internship mentors for guidance?
Yes, a chat option is available in your internship portal to connect with your mentors.
What is the difference between a completion letter and an experience letter?
A completion letter is issued when you complete your internship duration and the required learning modules in your portal. However, an experience letter requires you to submit your project work with details. Without a project submission, you will not receive an experience letter.
